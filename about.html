Our Mission

Empowering spinal cord injury survivors across Acadiana with mobility grants and accessibility services.

Why We Exist

Founded by someone who understands firsthand the challenges of spinal cord injury, Meauxbility bridges the gap between need and mobility. We provide critical grants for adaptive equipment and accessibility modifications that insurance won't cover.

Our Vision

A world where every spinal cord injury survivor in Acadiana has access to the mobility equipment and services they need to live independently and with dignity.
