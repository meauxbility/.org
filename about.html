Experience-Driven Recovery

Founded by Sam Primeaux after his 2017 paralysis, Meauxbility turns lived experience into a roadmap others can follow to recovery.

The Journey Begins

Sam's life changed forever, but instead of letting pain define him, he chose to transform it into purpose.

2020

Community Building

Recognizing the need for support, Sam began connecting with other survivors and building a community.

2024

Meauxbility Founded

Formalizing the mission to provide mobility grants and accessibility services across Acadiana.
