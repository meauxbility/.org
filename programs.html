Eligibility Requirements

Medical Requirements

  • Documented spinal cord injury diagnosis
  • Current medical records
  • Physician recommendation for equipment/services
  • Ongoing medical care plan

Geographic Requirements

  • Louisiana Acadiana region resident
  • Proof of residency (utility bill, lease, etc.)
  • Minimum 6 months residency
  • Ability to receive services in region

Financial Requirements

  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Income verification documents
  • Insurance coverage information
  • Other funding sources explored

Application Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Required documentation submitted
  • Consent for information sharing
  • Agreement to program terms
