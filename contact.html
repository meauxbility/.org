Frequently Asked Questions

Who qualifies for a grant?

Spinal cord injury survivors residing in Louisiana's Acadiana region who need mobility equipment or accessibility modifications not covered by insurance.

How long does the application process take?

We review applications within 7-10 business days and will contact you directly about next steps.

Can I volunteer with Meauxbility?

Yes! We're always looking for volunteers. Use the contact form above and select "Volunteer Interest" to get started.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes! Meauxbility is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.
