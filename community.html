Community Features

Peer Support Groups

Connect with others who understand your journey. Our support groups provide a safe space to share experiences, challenges, and victories.

  • Monthly in-person meetings
  • Virtual support sessions
  • Specialized groups by injury level
  • Family and caregiver support

Educational Workshops

Learn new skills, stay updated on technology, and discover resources that can improve your daily life and independence.

  • Technology training sessions
  • Health and wellness workshops
  • Financial planning guidance
  • Accessibility law updates

Social Events

Build friendships and enjoy life together through regular social activities, outings, and community celebrations.

  • Monthly social gatherings
  • Community outings
  • Holiday celebrations
  • Recreational activities

Mentorship Program

Connect with experienced survivors who can provide guidance, encouragement, and practical advice for your journey.

  • One-on-one mentoring
  • Group mentorship circles
  • Newly injured support
  • Career guidance
